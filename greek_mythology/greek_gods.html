Olympian Gods

In Greek Mythology back in the day the Greek believed there to be many gods for natural disasters and certain phenomonans. But the 12 main gods that we all know and learned thoughout our school life are called the Olympian Gods.The gods being:

  • Zeus
  • Hera
  • Poseidon
  • Demeter
  • Athena
  • Apollo
  • Artemis
  • Ares
  • Aphrodite
  • Hephaestus
  • Hermes
  • Dionysus
  • Hestia*

* Hestia gave up her seat in Olympias due to the constant bicker of the gods, which made her give up her seat to Dionysus, but she still has a home in Mount Olympus like all the other gods.
